    Computer Vision Route Detection

    This was our end of course project for the class, and we decided to work on climbing wall route detection. We were able to accomplish the detection of the individual tapes from a route as well as the color of the tape. For simpler images, we were able to obtain a 91% accuracy for detecting known tapes on the wall. For more complex images, we were able to obtain a 85% accuracy. As for the color detection, for simpler images, it had an average of 69% accuracy of correctly identifying the color of tape. For the complex image, it had an accuracy of 47%. You can read more about our results and process in our final report here: here
